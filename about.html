Our Head-Office Hauz Khas.

Coder World Institute, headquartered in Hauz Khas, stands as a beacon of innovation and excellence in the realm of coding education. Established in 2015 by the visionary founder, Prem Singh Negi, the institute has carved a niche for itself in nurturing the next generation of proficient coders. With state-of-the-art facilities and a dynamic curriculum designed to cater to the evolving demands of the tech industry, Coder World Institute is committed to empowering aspiring coders with the skills and knowledge necessary to thrive in the digital landscape. Since its inception, it has remained steadfast in its mission to cultivate a community of passionate and skilled coders, driving forward the advancements in technology and shaping the future of coding.

Faculty

At Code World, we pride ourselves on delivering an unparalleled coding education experience through our exceptional faculty members. Our instructors are not just educators; they are seasoned professionals with a passion for coding and a dedication to nurturing the next generation of software developers. Join us at Code World and learn from the best to become the best in the world of coding.
